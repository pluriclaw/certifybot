Grading System

Instant clarity on any connection

CertifyBot grades every endpoint A through F based on TLS configuration, cipher strength, and protocol support.

A
Excellent — modern TLS, strong ciphers, no issues
B
Good — minor config gaps, generally secure
C
Fair — outdated protocols, consider upgrading
D
Poor — weak ciphers or deprecated TLS in use
F
Fail — critical vulnerabilities, act immediately
Features

Everything you need to verify SSL

From raw protocol data to human-readable insights — CertifyBot covers the full surface.

🔐

Protocol Detection

Identifies TLS 1.3, 1.2, 1.1, and 1.0. Know exactly which protocol versions are active on any endpoint — and which legacy versions are still enabled.

🔑

Cipher Suite Analysis

Decodes every cipher in use, rates its strength from strong to weak, and flags deprecated or vulnerable cipher suites that could put your data at risk.

🔗

Certificate Chain Verification

Reconstructs the full chain from leaf to root CA. Validates signatures, checks subject/issuer relationships, and identifies missing or misordered intermediate certs.

📅

Expiration Tracking & Alerts

Shows precise not-before and not-after dates for every certificate. Flags upcoming expirations so you never get caught by an unexpected cert drop.

🌿

Chain of Trust Validation

Verifies that each certificate in the chain is signed by the next — all the way to a trusted root CA. Detects self-signed certs, unknown CAs, and trust path breaks.

🛡

Security Headers Check

Inspect HSTS, CSP, X-Frame-Options, X-Content-Type-Options, Referrer-Policy, and more. Get concrete guidance on which headers are missing and why they matter.
